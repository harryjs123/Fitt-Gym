About FITT GYM

FITT Gym is a berkshire based gym designed to help individuals achieve their fittness goals. Founded in 2014, FITT gym has been recognized as one of the top UK gym brands for it's high intensity workouts, results driven personal trainers and for being one of the most socialable gyms to go to. With a state of the art facility FITT gym makes your workout experince a comfortable one as well as endless equipment to train with.
